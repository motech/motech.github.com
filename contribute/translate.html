Translate MOTECH

We are using Transifex to manage MOTECH translations, which makes it easy for non-geeks to help. If you speak multiple languages and would like to help us make MOTECH multilingual, please start by checking out our translation page to determine whether your language(s) are on our list. If they are, please contact us and we'll add you to the appropriate translation team(s).
