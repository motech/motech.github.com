Join the MOTECH Community

Thank you for your interest in contributing to MOTECH. There are many different ways to get involved - regardless of your area of expertise and time commitment, there is almost certainly a useful way for you to contribute.

Ways to Contribute

  • Develop
    We need help developing new modules and our web-based UI for administering the system.
  • Document
    We would love to tell our story better - perhaps you can help. We need help improving our developer documentation.
  • Test
    We need to make sure our system is robust in order to be used effectively to improve health outcomes.
  • Design
    We have some big plans around making MOTECH extremely simple for end users to administer. We could really use some help firming up these plans and then delivering on them.
  • Translate
    Do you speak multiple languages? Sign up as a translator on Transifex and help us make MOTECH multi-lingual.

Do you think there's another way you can help that isn't listed above? Contact us and let's talk about how you might get involved.
