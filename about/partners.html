Development partners

Dimagi

Dimagi produces CommCare, a mobile phone form-based app which facilitates better interaction between health care workers and patients. The CommCare UI is a series of forms that walk the caregiver and patient through the information and questions to be covered for a specific appointment protocol. In addition to presenting information, CommCare can be used to input data about the patientâ€™s visit. CommCare runs on medium-low capability phones and higher end phones.

CommCareHQ is the server component which stores the data collected by the CommCare handset application. CommCareHQ communicates with MOTECH server to coordinate care between the two systems.

CommCare and MOTECH are currently partnering on work with CARE in Bihar. We expect to partner with them more as higher-end phones become more common.

InSTEDD

InSTEDD has several technogies which insulate application developers from the complexity of dealing with individual operator/aggregator IVR or SMS systems. We expect to partner with MOTECH in the future to provide simpler authoring of call/message flows.
