Integration with Other Health Systems

A key goal for MOTECH SUITE is to to integrate with existing systems where appropriate, including governmental or NGO databases, national ID systems, and other help systems.

Examples of MOTECH SUITE System Integration

  • * Ability to have bi-directional data transfer between OpenMRS and MOTECH SUITE.
  • * Integration of MOTECH FLW and patient data with India's Mother and Child Tracking System (MCTS). [In progress]
  • * Integration with DHIS2 in Ghana and Ethiopia. [In progress]
  • * Sharing patient registration and EMR data with World Health Partner's ReMeDi system.

MOTECH SUITE Components Related to Integration

  • * MRS Module
  • * OpenMRS Module
