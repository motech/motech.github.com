MOTECH Platform Roadmap

Starting with the "Salk" release in the Fall of 2012, MOTECH Platform will move to a cadence of monthly releases, with stable releases happening quarterly. The lists below mention some of the major features that we expect to implement in each coming release.

Our releases are named after physicians who have made major contributions to the advancement of medicine. Because current implementations of MOTECH are focused on maternal/child health, HIV/AIDS, and TB, and because we currently work in India and Africa, we may exhibit particular bias towards physicians who have worked in these areas when choosing namesakes for our releases.

Jonas Salk (1914-1995) is best known for his development of the first polio vaccine, a celebrated event in 1955 at the height of the worldwide polio epidemic.
