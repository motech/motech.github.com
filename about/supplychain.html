Supply Chain Logistics

MOTECH SUITE applications can deliver improved Supply Chain logistics through reporting, monitoring, and notification of stock levels, distribution inventory, and delivery status. The Supply Chain functionality can scale to complex scenarios and scale by combining Dimagi cStock, OpenLMIS, InSTEDD Resource Map, and MOTECH Platform..

Why Supply Chain is Important

Increased demand for health services is only effective if the needed supplies are at the point of care. Harnessing the ubiquity of mobile devices for reporting and messaging supply information can make the supply chain process more efficient

Key MOTECH SUITE Demand Generation Capabilities

  • * SMS-based reporting of invetory
  • * Large scale logistics coordination via OpenLMIS
  • * Supply delivery notifications via IVR or SMS ensure more efficient delivery
  • * Demand Generation messages can be tailored to the stock on hand at local clinic
