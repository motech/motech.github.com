What is MOTECH?

The MOTECH Suite is a set of Open Source technology components from a consortium of partners who have recognized that their complementary software efforts can address the core needs of mHealth. The suite can address a broad range of scenarios including Demand Generation (announcements, targeted messages, appointment reminders), Registration (medical records for patients, databases of facilities and front line health workers), Supply Chain Logistics (inventory management, stock alerts), Adherence Monitoring and Disease Management (treatment reminders, alerts, referrals, decision trees). By building on top of MOTECH Suite, NGOs and other health system implementors can more easily deliver solutions which imporve health outcomes. MOTECH Suite is currently being used in more than 30 different implementations in 15 countries.

MOTECH applications:

  • * Communicate information to patients via voice or SMS
  • * Customize messages based on a schedule of care prescribed for the patient—appointments, pill reminders, or vaccinations
  • * Collect data from patients or caregivers
  • * Receive reports of services delivered by caregiverse
  • * Alert caregivers to the status of their patients
  • * Coordinate messaging between differnt parts of the healthcare ecosystem

Learn more about the Features of MOTECH and the Current Implementations of MOTECH around the world.

MOTECH enables the creation of mHealth applications which close the loop between patients, caregivers, and health information systems by sending messages to patients and caregivers and coordinating information flow between different components of the health delivery system.

Event Driven

MOTECH's components are loosely coupled, so they can be easily updated and maintained.

Module Based

By using the OSGI framework, new features can be easily added to a MOTECH-based system.

OpenMRS Integration

MOTECH integrates with OpenMRS and other EMR systems.

Telephony Integration

The MOTECH API connects to many IVR and SMS services.

How to Build with MOTECH

MOTECH is a modular, event driven system written in Java. MOTECH is designed to be extensible, support additional modules of functionality, and scale to millions of users.

To learn how to develop a MOTECH application, begin with the Getting Started Guide and explore the Module and API Documentation and Demos.
