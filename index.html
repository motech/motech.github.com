What is MOTECH?

MOTECH is a software system from Grameen Foundation that harnesses the ubiquity of mobile phones to deliver and receive information from patients and caregivers. By building on top of MOTECH, NGOs and other health system implementers can more easily deliver solutions that lead to positive health outcomes. MOTECH-based solutions can be authored easily, scale to millions of users, and—where approriate—share data and tools with other MOTECH solutions or external systems.

MOTECH applications:

  • * Communicate information to patients via voice or SMS
  • * Customize messages based on a schedule of care prescribed for the patient—appointments, pill reminders, or vaccinations
  • * Collect data from patients or caregivers
  • * Receive reports of services delivered by caregivers
  • * Alert caregivers to the status of their patients
  • * Coordinate messaging between different parts of the healthcare ecosystem

Learn more about the Features of MOTECH and the Current Implementations of MOTECH around the world.

MOTECH enables the creation of mHealth applications which close the loop between patients, caregivers, and health information systems by sending messages to patients and caregivers and coordinating information flow between different components of the health delivery system.
