What MOTECH Does

MOTECH enables the creation of mHealth applications which close the loop between patients, caregivers, and health information systems by sending messages to patients and caregivers and coordinating information flow between different components of the health delivery system.

MOTECH applications can:

  • * Communicate information to patients via voice or SMS
  • * Customize messages based on a schedule of care prescribed for the patient—appointments, pill reminders, or vaccinations
  • * Collect data from patients or caregivers
  • * Receive reports of services delivered by caregiverse
  • * Alert caregivers to the status of their patients
  • * Coordinate messaging between differnt parts of the healthcare ecosystem

Learn more about the types of applications that can be built with MOTECH on our Implementations page.

Customized Messaging

Patients and caregivers are reminded of tasks at the appropriate time in the care schedule.

Broad Reach

MOTECH works with IVR or SMS to reach anyone with mobile phone access, regardless of literacy level.

Coordinated Care

MOTECH brings together different health systems to enable coordination and efficiency.

Event Driven

At it's core MOTECH defines and responds to events enabling loose coupling of components.

Module Based

MOTECH uses the OSGI framework allowing for new features to be bundled and added easily.

OpenMRS Integration

Out of the box MOTECH provides the ability to integrate with OpenMRS and other EMR systems.

Telephony Integration

MOTECH provides an API to IVR and SMS services allowing connection to different telephony providers.

How MOTECH Works

MOTECH is Open Source and written in Java. It is designed to be extensible to support additional modules of functionaltiy and scalable to support millions of patients.
